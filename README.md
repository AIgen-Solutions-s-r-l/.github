## About Us

**Algen Solutions** is an innovative Italian company specializing in artificial intelligence and machine learning solutions. We develop cutting-edge software systems that leverage AI to solve complex problems across various domains including finance, healthcare, data analytics, and business intelligence.

Our mission is to democratize access to advanced AI technologies, making them accessible and practical for businesses of all sizes.

## What We Do

- **AI/ML Solutions**: Custom machine learning models and AI-powered applications
- **Financial Technology**: Trading platforms, risk management, and automated financial systems
- **Data Analytics**: Advanced data processing, analysis, and visualization tools
- **Smart Automation**: Intelligent systems that learn and adapt to optimize business processes

## Our Projects

### [SpreadPilot](https://github.com/algensolutions/SpreadPilot)
A modern microservices-based trading platform designed for scalable and reliable options trading automation. Built with Python, PostgreSQL, and cloud-native technologies.

**Tech Stack**: Python | PostgreSQL | Microservices

### [HedgeLock](https://github.com/algensolutions/HedgeLock)
Decentralized loan monitoring and management system with automated liquidation protection. Ensures financial security through intelligent risk assessment and real-time monitoring.

**Tech Stack**: Python | Blockchain | Risk Management

### [TEA](https://github.com/algensolutions/TEA)
Technical Analysis and Event-driven Architecture system for financial markets. Provides real-time market analysis and trading signals.

**Tech Stack**: Python | Real-time Analytics

### [HRLForLiving](https://github.com/algensolutions/HRLForLiving)
A household resource learning (HRL) system that simulates and learns to optimally allocate monthly salary among investments, savings, and discretionary spending.

**Tech Stack**: Python | Reinforcement Learning | Financial Planning
